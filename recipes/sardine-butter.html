Sardine Butter

Description

A different butter to differ the daily sandwichs, with a lovable strong seafood flavor and a perfect texture.

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons of salted butter
  • 1 can of sardines
  • juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon of minced chives
  • ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place the butter in a bowl with the drained sardines. Use a fork to smash them together.
  2. Add the lemon juice after a minute. Mash until incorporating everything. Add the chives and black pepper last thing.
  3. Roll the butter in plastic and refrigerate for an hour
  4. Serve it with toast and have fun!
