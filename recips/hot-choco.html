Hot Chocolate

Description

A drink perfect for the cold days, with the sweetness and bitterness of the cocoa and marshmallow.

The definition of perfection!

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce of dark chocolate
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix the chocolate and cocoa powder and half the milk in a saucepan. Stir until the chocolate is melted;
  2. Add the rest of the milk and the salt and let it in the heat for a little bit;
  3. Stir the sugar and pur into a mug. Top with marshmallows if desired
